THE SCHOOL HISTORY
Welcome to Indus Early Learning Centre, started in January 2015 at Koramangala, Bangalore by the Indus Trust. The school caters to pre-primary education for children from the Age 1.5 Years to 6 Years.

CAMPUS IELC CAMPUS LIFE
Children grow into inquisitive, eager, and lifelong learners in a child-friendly atmosphere at the Indus Early Years Learning Centre (IELC). Here, we strike a balance between learning and having fun, and we offer plenty of opportunities for children to see themselves as the architects of their own worlds. With the appropriate tools and teachers who are enthusiastic about and dedicated to their work, we foster a lively and happy environment. Our teachers are driven, enthusiastic, and passionate about assisting each child in developing their special skills so that they will become "Leaders of Tomorrow" who are ready for any challenge. Our curriculum is play-based and nature-oriented so that children experience learning and work as enjoyable and interesting.

THE PARENT ADVISORY COMMITTEE (PAC)
Indus maintains an open line of communication with parents since we see them as participants in their children's education. In order to ensure that the best decisions are made with a shared objective in mind—to equip our children with the skills they need to thrive in life—this relationship is exceptional in that it fosters a caring atmosphere for the students. The Parent Advisory Committee (PAC) has established grade-specific groups for parents to communicate with one another and discuss issues pertaining to the school and their child's grade. A parent moderates each group. The school's open-door policy is supported by this initial purpose..
