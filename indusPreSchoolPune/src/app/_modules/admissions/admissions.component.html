ADMISSIONS At the Indus Early Learning Centre, Aundh, we believe in creating leaders of tomorrow by preparing children for an unforeseen future. While most students join school at the beginning of each academic year, we understand that some may not be able to do so. Based on the availability of seats, we are happy to admit students throughout the year.
ELIGIBILITY The School does not discriminate, based on race, creed, colour, gender or national origin. We do, however, limit the number of students based on our capacity to provide required learning support individually.
ADMISSION PROCESS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS 1. What is the timing of the school for children? Nursery- 9:30am to 12:30 pm
Reception to Prep 2- 9:00am to 2:00pm 2. What is the student teacher ratio? We maintain a healthy teacher-child ratio. Additionally, we have subject teachers and school attendants who support the teachers in the daily routine. The teacher-child ratio of all grade levels is as follows: Pre-Nursery-1:6, Nursery-1:10, Reception – 1:12, Prep I & II-1:14 3. What documents do I need to enroll my child at your pre-School? We need a photocopy of the child’s birth certificate, id proof of parents and 3 recent photographs of the child. Other details of the child have to be filled in the admission form that will be provided to you online or at the centre. 4. Do you provide transport? IELC provides safe and secure bus transport. The transport charge varies as per the distance and is not a part of the tuition fee. 5. Do you provide meals? Yes, we provide a very healthy and balanced meal to our students at an extra cost. 6. What curriculum do you follow? Being a part of Indus International School Pune, which is an IB school, we follow the IB curriculum for early years too. 7. Is there a transition period for my child? The first few days of school are known as “Phase-In.” During a transition period, children will meet their new teachers and classmates, and are gradually familiarized with school and classroom routines. Parents or guardians will receive timings for your child's transition. One parent/guardian is expected to be available for portions of Phase-In to meet their children’s teachers and to fill out appropriate forms. The length of time it takes before your child attends a full schedule will depend on the age and the individual child.
