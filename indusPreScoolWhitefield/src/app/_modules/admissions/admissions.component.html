ADMISSIONS At the Indus Early Learning Centre, Whitefield, we believe in creating leaders of tomorrow by preparing children for an unforeseen future. While most students join school at the beginning of each academic year, we understand that some may not be able to do so. Based on the availability of seats, we are happy to admit students throughout the year.
ELIGIBILITY The school does not discriminate, based on race, creed, colour, gender or national origin. We do, however, limit the number of students based on our capacity to provide required learning support individually.
