HAPPY ENVIRONMENT OUR INSTAWALL
From the CEO/ MD's desk LT. GENERAL ARJUN RAY, PVSM, VSM (RETD.)
“The 21st century is characterised by VUCA (volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity); by awesome acceleration and change; and obsolescence in knowledge, technology, jobs, and even in traditional leadership. The purpose of education will, therefore, be to prepare children for a future we do not know. This is the Indus vision. The The most effective way to make this a reality is to prepare our students to be life entrepreneurs. A life entrepreneur is a person who applies the principles of a startup in one’s personal and work life. Entrepreneurship is not just a business tool. It is a mindset that will help each one of us to ”
• Breakout of our comfort zone and into a growth and exponential zone. To get out of one’s comfort zone is doing things you don’t feel comfortable with doing.
• Take critical decisions in one’s personal and work life, with the ability to empathise, to identify problems, collaborate, and seek Big Data in decision-making.
• To prototype, experiment and validate breakthrough ideas.
• To set challenging goals.
